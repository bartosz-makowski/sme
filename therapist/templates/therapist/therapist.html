Bartosz Makowski

Having participated in various sports throughout my life I went through many injuries. By making my way to recovery from each of them I developed an interest in human anatomy, physiology and biomechanics. This empowered me to follow a path to the university to study Physiotherapy. After graduating I came to England and started working as a sports massage therapist in clinics in central London. Currently, I work as a Physiotherapy Assistant at Stoke Mandeville Hospital. Having practised bodywork for nearly 5 years I helped many people ranging from office workers to professional athletes overcome their injuries and allow them to feel better, move easier and aid them in reaching their goals.
